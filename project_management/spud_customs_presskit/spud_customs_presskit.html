Game Overview

Spud Customs is a game inspired by "Papers, Please" about managing a border checkpoint for potatoes, combining document inspection mechanics with missile defense action. As a customs officer, you'll examine passports, enforce changing immigration laws, and protect your border from runners attempting to cross illegally. Balance accuracy and speed to meet your daily quota while defending against threats in this quirky take on bureaucratic gameplay.

The 1.1 update brings significant enhancements to gameplay, performance, and localization, making the game more accessible and enjoyable for players worldwide.

Key Features

  • Examine potato passports and enforce ever-changing immigration laws
  • Defend your border from runners with a missile targeting system
  • Experience a blend of document inspection and action gameplay
  • Progress through an engaging story mode with colorful potato characters
  • Compete in Score Attack mode for high scores and leaderboard rankings
  • NEW IN 1.1: Multiple language support including French, German, Spanish and more
  • NEW IN 1.1: Enhanced performance and gameplay improvements
  • NEW IN 1.1: Improved UI and user experience

1.1 Update Highlights

The 1.1 update includes:

  • UI Improvements: Enhanced user interface and better feedback systems
  • Bug Fixes: Resolved known issues reported by our community

Developer Information

Lost Rabbit Digital is an independent game development studio founded in 2021. Our team is dedicated to creating unique gaming experiences that blend different genres and mechanics while maintaining a strong sense of humor and distinctive visual style.

Spud Customs represents our vision of combining bureaucratic simulation with action elements, wrapped in a quirky potato-themed world that offers both challenge and charm.
