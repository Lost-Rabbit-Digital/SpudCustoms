[h2]A Story of Choices and Consequences[/h2]

Across [b]10 story shifts[/b] and [b]22 decision points[/b], you'll shape the destiny of innocent (and not-so-innocent) potatoes. Your choices ripple through the narrative, leading to [b]8 distinct endings[/b] - from loyal servant of the regime to revolutionary hero, from tragic survivor to romantic savior.
