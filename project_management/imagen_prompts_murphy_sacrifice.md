## Style Guide

All images should maintain consistency with the existing Spud Customs art style:
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920x1080)
- **Tone:** Dystopian industrial, noir atmosphere, dramatic lighting
- **Color Palette:** Dark grays, industrial browns, purple (Root Reserve), red emergency lighting, occasional chartreuse for hope
- **Characters:** Anthropomorphic potatoes with human proportions, expressive faces
- **Setting:** Brutalist industrial architecture, Soviet-inspired propaganda aesthetic
